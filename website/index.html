Riverside Health Center has served our community for over 30 years, providing accessible, high-quality medical care to patients of all ages — from newborns to older adults.

About Us

Founded in 1993, Riverside Health Center began as a single-physician practice on the banks of Millbrook Creek. Today it is a multi-specialty clinic with more than 40 providers, two urgent care suites, an on-site laboratory, and a digital imaging center — all under one roof.

Our philosophy is simple: every patient deserves unhurried, attentive care in an environment that feels welcoming, not institutional. We accept most major insurance plans and offer a sliding-scale fee program for uninsured and underinsured patients.

We are accredited by The Joint Commission and recognised by the National Committee for Quality Assurance (NCQA) as a Patient-Centered Medical Home — a standard that puts coordinated, preventive care at the heart of everything we do.
